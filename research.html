Rutgers University Waksman Student Scholars Program

I worked on a project called Sequence Analysis of Clones from a Landoltia punctata cDNA Library at my high school biology lab. This project involved using a miniprep to extract DNA from bacterial cultures and doing a PCR to replicate the DNA to analyze the sequence using waveform software and searching for protein matches and determining the significance of that sequence. The mRNA sequence that I analyzed is in my publications.
